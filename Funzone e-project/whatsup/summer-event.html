Summer

A summer visit to FunZone ParK® is fun for the whole family! Every ticket is packed with thrilling coasters, an ENTIRE water park, ZooAmerica® North American Wildlife Park, and so much more!

Fun For Everyone

FunZone ParK® has something for everyone! From thrilling coasters to classic family rides to an ENTIRE water park, there are countless ways to ride the day away at FunZone ParK.

FunZone's Chocolatetown®

Enjoy rides on our biggest coaster ever, Candymonium®, delicious eats at our 3 dining experiences including The Chocolatier℠ Restaurant Bar + Patio, sweet souvenirs from our new retail shops, selfies in front of the iconic Kisses℠ Fountain, and more! Learn More

The Boardwalk At FunZone ParK®

Make a splash at the 11-acre water park inside FunZone ParK®! The Boardwalk is included with all summer admission tickets Memorial Day Weekend to Labor Day.

ZooAmerica North American Wildlife Park

ZooAmerica® is also included in your one-price FunZone ParK admission. Experience this world-class, accredited wildlife park that is home to more than 200 North American animals.
