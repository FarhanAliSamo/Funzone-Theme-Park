FunZone Halloween

Enjoy family fun and sweet treats while you stroll through Treatville, dance with your favorites at the Character Glow Dance Parties, experience lights out coasters, and don’t forget your flashlight for a tour through ZooAmerica®. Plus, experience thrilling Dark Nights Scare Zones - included in every Hersheypark® ticket.

Coasters & Rides For The Whole Family

FHappy Halloweeeeeee! Get ready for fall thrills on your favorite rides and 13 coasters. Plus, exclusively for Hersheypark Halloween, experience lights-out rides during the last hour of operation on Candymonium®, Comet, and Lightning Racer®. Laff Trakk will be dark during all operating hours.

Hershey's Character Glow Dance Party

Dance the night away with your favorite FunZone Characters. They’re all dressed up and ready to trick-or-treat, and party to the season’s classics under the glowing Founder’s Way lights!

Dark Nights Scare Zones

Guests can take on three Scare Zones ranging from eerie to intense, to experience a lead in for the full-scale fright of our four haunted houses. Take on Midway of Misery, Valley of Fear, and Darkstone’s Hollow..

Creatures Of The Night

See some of our creepy crawly animals during their favorite time of day, night! PS. don’t forget your flashlight!.
